About

I am currently a web development student studying the MERN stack at the University of Denver. I feelance - mostly using wordpress and bootstrap. Although my background is in graphic design, particularly image manipulation, front end work has become my passion.I offer premium services at an affordable price to small and medium sized businesses.

Design / Manipulation

Photo Manipulation is the process of editing a photograph in such an extreme way that it takes on an entirely different look. I often use Paint Shop Pro along with PhotoShop to combine various elements from several photographs and other resources to blend reality with illusion to create something truly remarkable. I love to experiment with colors and textures. Outside of manipulation, I also do light logo design and print design.

Web Development / Design

My web development service includes web design, web content development and client-side/server-side scripting, among other tasks. I specialize in Wordpress and Bootstrap development. My typical process goes as follows: Planning, Design, Development, Launch and Post-Launch. Outlining my basic process as a freelancer is by far the most important step I take with potential or new clients. A quick, high-level “This is how it works” discussion provides a framework for the entire project.
